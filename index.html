FAQ
Questions We Actually Get
Will callers know they're talking to AI? +

Most don't. Sarah is built on Grok ‚Äî xAI's voice engine that powers Tesla's in-car assistant. The response time is under 700ms and the voice is natural. We've had people ask to speak to "the girl who answered."

How does emotion detection work? +

Sarah analyzes keywords, speech patterns, punctuation, and vocal cues in real-time to score anger, frustration, urgency, confusion, and happiness. If a caller is upset, you get an instant escalation alert in your dashboard ‚Äî before the call even ends.

Which languages does Sarah speak? +

Sarah auto-detects and switches between 12 languages: English, French, Spanish, Mandarin, Cantonese, Punjabi, Hindi, Arabic, Portuguese, Korean, Japanese, and German. No setup needed ‚Äî she detects the language from the caller's first words.

What's the Live Whisper feature? +

While Sarah is on a live call, you can type a message from your dashboard ‚Äî and Sarah will weave it naturally into the conversation. It's like coaching your receptionist in real time without the caller knowing.

How long does setup take? +

About 48 hours. You fill in your business details ‚Äî name, hours, services, personality ‚Äî and we train the AI on your specific business. Then forward your phone number. That's it. We handle the setup and walk you through everything.

What if a caller wants a real person? +

Sarah can warm-transfer to you or your team. If unavailable, she takes a message, books a callback, or sends an SMS. No one falls through the cracks.

Can I connect to my CRM / Zapier? +

Yes. We have 6 pre-built webhook event types (call completed, appointment booked, emotion escalation, and more) with HMAC-signed payloads that work with Zapier, Make, n8n, or any custom integration.

Is my data private and secure? +

All data is stored securely on Canadian servers and accessible only to you. Enterprise tier includes HIPAA compliance mode with auto-PII redaction and audit logging for medical practices.
